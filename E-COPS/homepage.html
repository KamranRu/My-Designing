WELCOME TO E-COPS

Our aim by using this project is to develop E-cops.This system is easily accessible by all normal public,the police department & the Administrative department. This system is developed because the normal public is afraid to report a complaint against any crime going around them.so by using this system the normal public can easily report a complaint against any dangerous criminals to their respective police station. This police station share information with different police stations. Which help them in catching criminals keeping records of all criminals will help police to keep tab on criminal to perform legal activities. By using this system the record work is reduced and the data stored in complaines, status of complaines and other information. Form the whole,this method helps to bring general public and police department close to each other to share their views and it also reduces criminal activities.
