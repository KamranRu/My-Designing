Intelligent Process Landscape

The general understanding amongst organizations is that an enterprise software should provide seamless integration within its functional departments. But this is not enough. This understanding of a defined organizational process, is one that requires information flow to move smoothly within the hierarchical construct of the organization. This is a common short sighted perception amongst software applications. Our years of research shows that at a later maturity stage of a process the enterprise applications will have serious implications of a Silo Effect,wherein important decisions tend to move up unnoticed to sub branches of tertiary trees, unattended with no stakeholders to fulfill the same. Keeping with this research work we have created a unique intelligent process landscape that negates this Silo Effect.
